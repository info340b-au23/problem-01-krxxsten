Keroppi

Keroppi (Keh-row-pip-ee) is a green frog with big eyes! He is the smallest out of the Sanrio characters. Keroppi lives among his family, which consists of his brother, sister, and parents. A frog like Keroppi, loves to live by the bluest pond, which is why he reisdes by the Donut pond. His favorite activites include playing games and baseball!
